About Bogosort

Bogosort, also known as "bogus sort" or "shotgun sort", is an algorithm that sorts things by randomly shuffling all of the items to be sorted, then checks if it's sorted. This process repeats ad infinitum, until the items are in order. I chose the title, in hopes of convincing people that I might have a sense of humor, or at the very least, a rather odd one.
